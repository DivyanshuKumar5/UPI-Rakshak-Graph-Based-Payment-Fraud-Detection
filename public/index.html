// Threat Intelligence
Common UPI Fraud Patterns

UPI Rakshak detects all major fraud vectors active in India's digital payment ecosystem.

🕵️

Mule Account Rings

Fraudsters bounce stolen money through 10–50 fake accounts to obscure origin before final withdrawal.

⬤ CRITICAL RISK
📞

Vishing / OTP Scams

Caller poses as bank/NPCI official, tricks victim into sharing OTP or approving a collect request.

⬤ HIGH RISK
🔗

Phishing QR Codes

Malicious QR triggers a payment request (debit) instead of receive. Scanning drains your account.

⬤ HIGH RISK
📱

SIM Swap Fraud

Fraudster ports your number to a new SIM, receives all OTPs, and drains your linked bank account.

⬤ CRITICAL RISK
💰

Fake Merchant QR

Counterfeit QR stickers placed over real merchant codes. You pay the fraudster, not the shop.

⬤ MEDIUM RISK
🤖

Burst Micro-Attacks

Bots test stolen UPI credentials with ₹1–₹9 transfers before executing large withdrawals.

⬤ HIGH RISK
// Protection Guide
Stay Safe from UPI Fraud

9 RBI-recommended rules to protect your UPI accounts and digital wallet.

01

Never Share OTP or PIN

Your bank will NEVER ask for OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV over call or SMS. Hang up immediately.

02

Verify QR Before Scanning

Check if a QR sticker is placed over another. Manually type UPI ID for large transactions.

03

Set Daily UPI Limits

In your UPI app settings, set a daily limit (₹10K–₹25K) to cap potential damage from breach.

04

Enable Transaction Alerts

Turn on real-time SMS + email alerts for every UPI transaction. Instant detection = faster recovery.

05

Collect Request = Sending Money

Approving a "collect" request sends money OUT. Never approve unknown collect requests ever.

06

Use Biometric App Lock

Enable fingerprint/face unlock on your UPI app. Never store UPI PIN in notes or messages.

07

Verify Recipient Name

Always confirm the name shown by the UPI app matches who you're paying before proceeding.

08

Report Fraud in 24 Hours

Call 1930 (Cyber Crime Helpline) within 24 hours. Early reporting greatly increases fund recovery.

09

Revoke Unnecessary App Permissions

Remove SMS/call permissions from apps that don't need them. Malware reads OTPs via SMS access.
